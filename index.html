Famous Landmarks

New York City is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

  • The Statue of Liberty
  • Central Park
  • Times Square

Be sure to visit these must-see spots during your stay!

Top Tourist Activities

There are plenty of things to do in New York City:

  1. Take a walk through Central Park.
  2. Catch a Broadway show in the Theater District.
  3. Visit world-class museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Fun Facts About New York City

Did you know?

  • New York City is made up of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island.
  • Times Square is named after The New York Times newspaper.
  • Grand Central Terminal has a hidden tennis court!
